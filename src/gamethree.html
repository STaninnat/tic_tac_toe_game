Tic Tac Toe Game 3x3

Player 1 is X

Player 2 is O

Objective: Be the first player to get 3 of your marks (X or O) in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Setup: A 3x3 grid.

Gameplay: Two players take turns to place their mark (X or O) in an empty cell. The game continues until one player gets 3 in a row or all cells are filled.

Winning: The first player to get 3 marks in a row wins.

Draw: If all cells are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a draw.
